# Extra SC-200 Concepts

> Concepts that span multiple domains or appear inconsistently in the exam outline but show up on the test.

## Entity pages

Defender XDR + Sentinel both surface entity pages (user, device, IP, URL) with timeline + risk + insights. Use them as a starting point during triage.

## Sentinel content hub

Solutions catalog - one-click installs of connectors + analytics rules + workbooks for common products (Okta, AWS, GCP, Cisco, etc.).

## Sentinel pricing tiers

Pay-as-you-go vs commitment tiers (100GB/200GB/.../5TB/day). Commitment saves up to 65% vs PAYG.

## Defender XDR -> Sentinel migration of incidents

Bi-directional sync after enabling the connector and unifying the portal. Comments and status flow both ways.
